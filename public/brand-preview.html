05 / Iconography

Icon Style

Stroke-only SVG icons. Clean, geometric, minimal. Consistent 1.5px stroke width with round caps and joins.

Sites
Commerce
AI
ERP

Stroke Properties

All icons use stroke-width: 1.5 with stroke-linecap: round and stroke-linejoin: round. No filled icons.

Size & Container

Icons sit in a 44x44px container with a thin border (1.5px solid var(--surface)) and 10px border-radius. Icon itself is 22px.

Color

Default: var(--text) (#1A1714). On accent backgrounds: white. Active/hover: var(--accent).

No AI Slop

Never use gradient blobs, 3D renders, stock illustrations, or AI-generated imagery. Keep it geometric, clean, and intentional.

07 / Voice & Tone

How We Speak

Warm, direct, confident without arrogance. We speak like a skilled craftsman who respects both the work and the client.

Do

"We build ideas that seemed impossible."
"Every project is crafted with soul."
"Honest pricing, no markups."

Don't

"Leveraging cutting-edge synergies to deliver transformative solutions."
"We're the #1 best agency in the market!"
"Our robust, scalable, enterprise-grade platform..."

Tone Principles

Human, not corporate

Write as if talking to a friend who asked about your work. Use "we" and "you", not "the company" and "the client".

Confident, not boastful

Show competence through specifics, not superlatives. "6+ years in Salesforce" beats "leading experts".

Simple language

Short sentences. Active voice. No buzzwords. If a word doesn't add meaning, cut it.

Soul as differentiator

"With soul" isn't a slogan — it's the operating principle. Every touchpoint should feel intentional and cared for.

08 / Principles

Design Principles

01

Warm, Not Cold

Cream over white, terracotta over blue. Every color, texture, and shadow should feel organic and inviting, like a well-lit workshop.

02

Space to Breathe

Generous padding (7rem+ sections), uncrowded layouts, and deliberate emptiness. Whitespace is not wasted — it's what makes everything else work.

03

Subtle Borders

Thin 1px borders in warm tones. No heavy shadows or hard edges. Depth comes from layering and gentle contrast.

04

Micro-Motion

Smooth scroll reveals, gentle hovers, ease-in transitions. Nothing jumps, flashes, or bounces. Movement should feel like breathing.

05

Clean Iconography

SVG stroke-only, 1.5px lines, round caps. Geometric and intentional. No decorative flourishes that don't serve understanding.

06

No AI Slop

No gradient blobs, no 3D renders, no stock illustrations, no generic AI imagery. If it looks like a template, it doesn't belong here.

07

Craft Over Cleverness

Prefer the well-executed simple solution over the technically impressive complex one. Every element should feel considered, not clever.
